About Me

My name is Annalisa Wells and my first web design projects take us back to the early 2000s when I taught myself HTML and the basics of CSS and graphic design so that my Neopets could have super cool pet pages. I created and managed different Virtual Stable hobby groups, and I remember scouring the internet for PHP tutorials to try and figure out how to make my life easier instead of having to manually update how many horses a person had entered into a certain show. Good old middle school.

Fast forward quite a few years and, sadly, I had left web design off to one side (in part, I blame having the slowest Internet known to man) and instead pursued other creative passions and hobbies. After graduating from college with a B.A. in Cross-Cultural Studies/History, I somewhat reluctantly took on admin, retail, sales, and interior design jobs. Eventually, I found myself down a path that no longer suited me. While I was honing all things related to people and project management, I missed the feeling of creating and trackling hands-on problems.

In the summer of 2018, I decided to sit down and teach myself to code. Since then, I have diligently applied myself to learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as other peripheral languages and frameworks to round out my knowledge and understanding of the fundamentals and pllication of coding.

I look forward to putting my hard work, determination, and creativity to good use soon as a Front End Web Developer and Web Designer!
